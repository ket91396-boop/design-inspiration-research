Design Inspiration Research

A Codex skill that turns a loose design brief into sourced references, visual deconstruction, practical directions, and AI image prompts that match the final artifact.

View Skill GitHub Repository
Keywords

Logo, poster, typography, texture, wallpaper, layout, color, illustration, and web/UI are all scanned.

References

Routes searches to Behance, Dribbble, Pinterest, Fonts In Use, Typewolf, Unsplash, Pexels, Awwwards, Mobbin, and Land-book.

Output

Visual analysis, 3-5 actionable directions, and AI prompts tailored to posters, logos, lettering, textures, or UI.

Sourced, Not Vague

Each reference is linked and explained by what to borrow and what not to copy.

Designer-Friendly

References are translated into composition, type, color, texture, motif, and mood.

Use It In Codex

Invoke with $design-inspiration-research and describe the design you want to explore.
